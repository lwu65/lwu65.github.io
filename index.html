Winter Break 2016

In early January, I went on a trip to Minneapolis and Saint Paul. Although the weather was freezing cold, I still enjoyed the vacation.

The following are some places I visited and some photos:

Minneapolis

  • Minnehaha Park
  • Minneapolis Institute of Art
  • University of Minnesota
  • Minneapolis Sculpture Garden

Saint Paul

  • Cathedral of St. Paul
  • Minnesota State Capitol
  • Rice Park

I also went to Mall of America, located in Bloomington, MN. It takes about 20 mintutes to drive from downtown Minneapolis to the mall.
One can also get there by light rail or bus.
